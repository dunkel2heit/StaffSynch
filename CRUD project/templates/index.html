About StaffSync

StaffSync is an Employee Management System designed to efficiently manage employee information. The system allows administrators to create, view, update, and delete employee records while maintaining a centralized database. It provides an organized way to track employee details such as personal information, job positions, departments and salaries, improving workforce management and administrative efficiency.
